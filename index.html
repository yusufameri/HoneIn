What We Do

We are providing a connection with resources for High School Juniors and Seniors to be in direct contact with university undergraduates to ask questions about how they choose their prospective majors and discovered their passion. One of the biggest problems with our K-12 system in the US is that it fails largely in helping students discover their passion (and major) before they get into college/university. Every year, 20,000 students who apply to UMD change their major and an estimate 4 out of 5 people are changing their majors nationwide. By putting high school students in direct contact with university students, we are aiming to help lower this statistic and help students find their majors quicker and through a happier and less costly process than through delaying their opportunities in college.

Who We Are

We are a team of freshman in the Honors College who want to help alleviate the stress in the process of other upcoming freshman when they choose their majors. We know how dificult it is finding the right path and we are here and open to help in any way that we can!

How We Help

We offer mentorship, guidance, and real people that upcoming freshman can talk to about their college major questions! In addition, we also provide resources from UMD in which high schoolers can read and learn more about what its like in the life of a specific major and more specifically, what majors are offered here at Maryland.

Have any questions?.

Send us an email and we will be sure and quick to respond! We have a team of students and mentors ready to answer any question that you may throw at us.
